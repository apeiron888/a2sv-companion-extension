## Extension name
A2SV Companion

## Short description (max 132 chars)
Track A2SV progress and auto-submit accepted LeetCode/Codeforces solutions to your A2SV backend.

## Detailed description
A2SV Companion helps A2SV members streamline progress tracking and submission workflows.

Key features:
- Detects accepted LeetCode and Codeforces submissions
- Sends accepted solutions to the A2SV backend
- Works on problem and submission pages
- Lightweight and focused on A2SV workflows

Notes:
- Requires access to LeetCode and Codeforces pages to detect accepted solutions.
- Sends data to the A2SV backend for storage and processing.
